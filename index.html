Hi, I’m a UX designer based in Cracow, passionate about Cross Cultural and Speculative design.

MY DESIGN DNA

UX Design

I design experiences that just work — seamless, intuitive, and purposeful. With a foundation in design thinking and the double diamond model, I build flows that serve both user intent and business logic. Lately, I’ve been exploring a shift from user-centered to practice-centered design — focusing on what people do, not just what they say they need.

UX Research

I combine data and empathy to uncover what really matters. Whether through interviews, ethnographic fieldwork, or experimental tools like Wizard of Oz, my research bridges the gap between what users expect and what businesses need to deliver. I believe in insight that sparks action.

Cross-Cultural

Cultural nuance changes everything. Working across borders taught me how design needs to shift with the people it serves — from layout expectations to what “intuitive” really means. I make sure products feel local, even when they’re global.

Service Design

Good service design is invisible—it just flows. Trained by the Nielsen Norman Group and shaped by academic and industry projects, I approach service design as a full-journey discipline. My focus? Smooth touchpoints, reduced friction, and experiences that work online, offline, and in between.

ABOUT

I see myself as a flâneur, wandering around the city and uncovering its hidden corners from the back seat of a local.

Similarly, in the research process, I immerse myself in the user’s perspective, exploring their journey with the same curiosity and attentiveness.
