Grounded in Norman Nielsen Group training, university research, and industry projects, my approach to service design focuses on the entire journey—identifying pain points, streamlining processes, and enhancing experiences at every touchpoint. To me, service design isn’t just about fixing issues; it’s about crafting solutions that work seamlessly across digital and physical spaces.
